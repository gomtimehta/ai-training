## Objective
Create AI trainings for different types of roles in the SDLC as a starting point.

# Why we are doing this.
AI is transforming every area of technology. We want to leverage this moment to use our expertise to train professionals on how to use AI effectively and, in turn, drive revenue growth.

# Goals
Maximize audience - by end of 3 months we want to have 50 people who want to continue coming back and can promote our sessions.

First 6 months goal - Generate $500 per month from training delivery.

Must deliver 30 minutes session weekly. Session is recorded and uploaded on YouTube.

# How we will achieve this
Define advanced topics and a 4-week agenda
Deliver weekly 30-minute sessions focused on real-world use cases
Launch social media channels (YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, X)
Publish each session across platforms
Use short-form content (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube Shorts) to drive traffic to full YouTube videos; post full videos on YouTube and X.
User registration and marketing
- LinkedIn training user registration form
- On social media - promote links for user registration
- Questionaire - AI skill assessment
- Social media and direct marketing and creating new leads.
